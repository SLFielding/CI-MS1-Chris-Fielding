About

I’ve been recording music full time for almost 20 years, in that time I’ve worked with countless bands and solo artists. I take great pleasure in helping artists to realise their potential and create the music they hear in their head. Whilst I am also passionate about musical and recording equipment - I am much more interested in creating an atmosphere where a musician can perform to their best and not have to worry about the technical side of recording. As a touring musician myself that has played all over the world, I understand how bands and artists work and I try to recreate that feeling in the recording studio.
